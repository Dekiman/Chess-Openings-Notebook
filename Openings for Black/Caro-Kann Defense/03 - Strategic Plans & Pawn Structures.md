# Strategic Plans & Pawn Structures

## Black's Goals
- **Light-squared Bishop**: Develop the bishop to f5 or g4 *before* playing ...e6.
- **Solid Pawn Structure**: Maintaining a solid structure and challenging White's center later (often with ...c5).
- **Endgame Focus**: Trading off pieces to reach an endgame where structural advantages matter more.

## White's Goals
- **Space Advantage**: Especially in the **Advance Variation**, White looks to restrict Black's movement.
- **Tactical Pressure**: In the **Panov-Botvinnik Attack**, White accepts an Isolated Queen's Pawn (IQP) for dynamic piece activity and attacking chances.
