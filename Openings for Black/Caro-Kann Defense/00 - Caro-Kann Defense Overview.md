# Caro-Kann Defense Overview

## Philosophy
The Caro-Kann Defense is one of Black's most reliable and solid responses to 1. e4. Known for its structural integrity, it often leads to endgames where Black's solid pawn structure provides a slight advantage. Unlike the French Defense, Black's light-squared bishop is not blocked in by ...e6.
