# King's Indian Defense Overview

**ECO Codes:** E60-E99

**Philosophy:** Hypermodern defense where Black allows White a strong center, planning to counter-attack it later.

## Main Counter
The **Samisch Variation (5. f3)** or the **Four Pawns Attack (5. f4)**. White builds a massive center and prepares for a kingside storm.
