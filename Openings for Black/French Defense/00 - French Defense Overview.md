# French Defense Overview

**ECO Codes:** C00-C19

**Philosophy:** Solid, counter-attacking opening where Black fights for the center with e6 and d5.

## Main Counter
The **Advance Variation (3. e5)** or the **Tarrasch Variation (3. Nd2)**. White seeks to control the center and limit Black's light-squared bishop.
