# Sicilian Defense Overview

**ECO Codes:** B20-B99

**Philosophy:** Black fights for the center asymmetrically, creating unbalanced and dynamic positions.

## Main Counter
The **Open Sicilian (3. d4)** or the **Alapin Variation (3. c3)**. White seeks dynamic piece play or a solid central presence.
