# Queen's Gambit Overview

**ECO Codes:** D06-D69

**Philosophy:** White offers the c-pawn to deflect Black's d-pawn, aiming for total central control and powerful development. It is one of the most prestigious and deeply studied openings for White.

The **Slav Defense (2... c6)** or the **QGD (2... e6)**. Black aims for a solid structure and eventual equalization.
