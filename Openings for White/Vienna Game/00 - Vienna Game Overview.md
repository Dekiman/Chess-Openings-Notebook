# Vienna Game Overview

**ECO Codes:** C25-C29

**Philosophy:** A flexible alternative to the Ruy Lopez and Italian Game. White develops the Nc3 before Nf3, keeping options open for f4 (Vienna Gambit) or g3 (Mieses Variation).

The **Falkbeer Variation (2... Nf6)**. Black immediately develops and prepares for the d5 break.
